Pringles

At one point in your life you've placed two pringles back sides together and pretended to be a duck. We've all been there. We've all been silly! Pringles are known for their iconic, legendendary flavors: sour cream and onion. Now, we're not saying that this is the best flavor... but it is the best flavor. Pringles is an American potato chip brand with an interesting history. It is currently owned by Kellog, the company responsible for many cereal brands. Originally, the brand belonged to Procter and Gamble. They created the brand in order to address the public's complaints over broken, greasy, stale, potato chips. Chemist Fredric Baur was the original mastermind behind the snack brand. He found that by utilizing a potato dough, it was easier to prevent a stale texture. The chips were baked to perfection with the right amount of crunch!
